Pizza Euphoria

The site name chosen for this project is Pizza Euphoria.

Reason: This name conveys a sense of joy and satisfaction associated with enjoying delicious pizza.

Site Purpose

Pizza Euphoria aims to provide a platform for customers to explore our menu offerings, learn about our pizzeria, and easily get in touch with us.

Scenarios

  • What are the specialty pizzas offered by Pizza Euphoria?
  • How can I place an order for delivery?

Color Schema

The selected color scheme for Pizza Euphoria:

  • Main Color: #FFA500 (orange) - used for headings and accents
  • Secondary Color: #FFFFFF (white) - used for backgrounds

Typography

Selected fonts and their usage:

  • Main Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif (for headings and body text)
